## What is odio?

odio is what happens when an R&D engineer spends 15 years refusing vendor lockin and letting good hardware die.

odio is an open streamer. Not a locked appliance: a platform. A commercial-grade user experience based on free software running on hardware people thought obsolete.
Open source down to the last line, open to the outside through a REST API that makes every node controllable, observable, and fully integrated into your smart home.

MPD, Snapcast, Shairport Sync, BlueZ, upmpdcli: the best open-source audio daemons, orchestrated as one coherent system. Controllable from a phone, a browser, or Home Assistant.

Built for makers. The API is there for you to build on: custom clients, automations, integrations no one has thought of yet. Your setup, your rules, your next idea.
