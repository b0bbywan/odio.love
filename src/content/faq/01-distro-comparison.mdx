odio is not a custom distro: it's a stack of services that installs on top of your existing Raspberry Pi OS Lite. It runs on every Pi from the original B (700 MHz single core, 32-bit) to the Pi 5, exposes power and Bluetooth as [Home Assistant entities](#features), plays inserted CDs and USB drives automatically with metadata, and ships a [documented HTTP API](https://docs.odio.love) you can build your own UIs on. See [how it stacks up against commercial alternatives](#comparison).
